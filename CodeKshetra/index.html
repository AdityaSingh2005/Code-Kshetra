About Code-क्षेत्र

Welcome to Code-क्षेत्र, a thrilling 36-hour hackathon hosted at JIMS Rohini Sector-5, a prestigious institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and holistic development, in February 2024. More than just a competition, it's an immersive experience filled with innovation, exciting prizes, swags, and a lot of fun. It's your opportunity to connect with experienced mentors and judges.

Organized by Geek Room, a vibrant community dedicated to enhancing coding skills, started as an open community for solving tech-related queries and participating in college competitions. In a short span, it has grown to include 6000+ participants from colleges across India. Now, launching chapters in different colleges, we're excited to have it at JIMS!

Join us on this exciting coding journey and become a part of Geek Room's JIMS Chapter. Connect with us on Geek Room's JIMS Chapter.

F.A.Q

  • What is Code Kshetra and what makes it unique?

    Code Kshetra is a groundbreaking hackathon jointly organized by JIMS Sector-5 Rohini and Geek Room. It provides a unique experience, redefining the boundaries of creativity and technology, transcending conventional hackathons.

  • When and where is Code Kshetra 2024 taking place?

    Code Kshetra 2024 is an offline event scheduled for 5th-6th February. The venue is JIMS Sector-5 Rohini, near Rithala Metro Station . It's a 36-hour hackathon that promises an immersive experience in tech innovation.

  • What is the prize pool for Code Kshetra, and how are the prizes distributed?

    Code Kshetra boasts a prize pool worth INR 3,00,000+, with a cash prize worth INR 25,000+. Details about the prize distribution will be announced closer to the event.

  • What engaging activities can participants expect during Code Kshetra?

    Code Kshetra features live project presentations, idea pitching, judging by esteemed panels, and a fun and games round with prizes to keep participants engaged throughout the hackathon.

  • What exclusive perks are provided to participants?

    Participants at Code Kshetra enjoy exclusive perks, including free swags, networking opportunities with industry experts, goodies, accommodation, meals, and much more, enhancing their overall experience.

  • How does Code Kshetra encourage innovation and problem-solving?

    Code Kshetra challenges participants to push their boundaries and foster inventive solutions to real-world issues. It celebrates the union of innovation and technology, providing a platform for participants to showcase their skills.

  • Who are the organizers of Code Kshetra, and what is their mission?

    Code Kshetra is hosted by the collaborative efforts of JIMS Sector-5 Rohini and Geek Room. Their mission is to provide a platform for tech enthusiasts to showcase their skills and amplify their potential through an exciting blend of competition, learning, and groundbreaking innovation.

  • How can participants connect with like-minded individuals and industry experts during Code Kshetra?

    Participants can connect with like-minded individuals and industry experts through networking sessions, project collaborations, and various interactive opportunities provided during Code Kshetra. It's a unique chance to learn, compete, and establish valuable connections.

  • Is there a code of conduct for participants?

    Yes, participants are required to follow the code of conduct outlined at [Code of Conduct]. It ensures a positive and respectful environment for all attendees.

  • How can I stay updated on Code Kshetra announcements and details?

    Stay updated on Code Kshetra announcements and details by regularly checking the official website. Additionally, follow their social media channels for the latest news and updates regarding the event.

  • How can I register for Code Kshetra 2024?

    To register for Code Kshetra 2024, visit the official website and follow the registration instructions. The registration link on Devfolio can be found [here]. Complete the registration form and make sure to adhere to the specified deadlines.

  • What is the maximum team size allowed for Code Kshetra?

    The maximum team size allowed for Code Kshetra is 4 members. Participants can form teams with a minimum of 1 member and a maximum of 4 members.

  • Can I participate as an individual, or is it mandatory to be part of a team?

    Code Kshetra allows both individual and team participation. You can register as an individual and participate solo, or you can form a team with up to 3 other members.

  • Are there any specific requirements for the projects that participants can work on?

    Participants in Code Kshetra have the flexibility to choose the type of projects they want to work on. There are no specific requirements, allowing participants to showcase their creativity and skills by working on innovative solutions to real-world issues.

  • What should I bring to Code Kshetra, and what will be provided by the organizers?

    Participants are encouraged to bring their laptops and any specific tools or hardware they may need for coding. Organizers will provide the necessary infrastructure, but participants should ensure they have everything they need for a successful hacking experience.
