Code of Conduct

Guidelines and rules to ensure a positive and inclusive environment during Code Kshetra Hackathon.

Welcome to Code Kshetra Hackathon! We value and prioritize a positive, inclusive, and collaborative environment for all participants. To ensure a successful and respectful event, please adhere to the following Code of Conduct:

  1. Respect Everyone: Treat all participants, organizers, sponsors, and staff with courtesy and respect. Harassment or discrimination based on gender, race, ethnicity, age, religion, or any other factor will not be tolerated.
  2. Inclusive Environment: Foster an inclusive atmosphere where everyone feels welcome. Embrace diverse perspectives, experiences, and backgrounds.
  3. Collaboration over Competition: Encourage collaboration and sharing of ideas. This is a community event, and we believe in supporting each other to create innovative solutions.
  4. Intellectual Property: Respect intellectual property rights. Do not use or reproduce others' work without proper attribution or permission.
  5. Safe Space: Maintain a safe and harassment-free space for all participants. If you experience or witness any form of harassment, report it to the organizers immediately.
  6. Compliance with Rules: Adhere to the rules and guidelines provided by the organizers. Failure to comply may result in disqualification.
  7. Professionalism: Conduct yourself in a professional manner. This includes language, behavior, and interactions with others.
  8. Environment Preservation: Be mindful of your surroundings. Dispose of waste responsibly and respect the venue's rules.
  9. Open Communication: Communicate openly and constructively. Share your ideas, ask for help when needed, and be receptive to feedback.
  10. Abide by the Timeline: Adhere to the schedule provided by the organizers. Timely completion of tasks ensures a smooth and fair competition.
  11. Code Review: Be prepared for code reviews. Organizers may examine code to ensure compliance with rules and fair play.
  12. Enjoy and Learn: Participate with a positive mindset. Embrace the challenges, learn from the experience, and enjoy the process of creating something meaningful.

By participating in Code Kshetra Hackathon, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct. Organizers reserve the right to take appropriate action, including disqualification, for any behavior that violates these guidelines.

Let's make Code Kshetra Hackathon a memorable and enriching experience for everyone!
